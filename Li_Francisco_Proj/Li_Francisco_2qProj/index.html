Introduction

For a long time, humanity, or at least much of the human population, have been entertaining the idea of having a "sustainable" way of life. We now are starting to think more about how our actions, practices and traditions like deforestation and excessive production of greenhouse gases affect the environment and all living beings that reside it. One example of this is the number of inventions that are capable of running on renewable sources of power which is in response to the threats imposed by global warming and the fact that the sources of energy that we are currently relying on (i.e.: crude oil, fossil fuels, etc.) are non-renewable and will take the planet millions of years to produce a fraction of it back. This pushed humanity to advocate the use sustainable energy and limiting the use of non-biodegradable materials such as plastic. These chain of events led to the inception of paper straws. Despite the bright and simple idea behind it: to create a biodegradable alternative to plastic straws, its many flaws not only make them infuriating to use; in fact, they are outright unusable.

The website's purpose

This website will be discussing the current problems of paper straws and several things that can be done to remove its problems or at least improve some aspects of it.
